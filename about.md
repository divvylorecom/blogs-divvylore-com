The **Divvylore Journal** is the editorial home of Divvylore. We publish
explainers, analysis and opinion on investing, personal finance and the
technology reshaping how India saves and grows its money.

Our promise is simple: plain language, honest framing, and links you can trust.
Where we mention a company, regulator or data source, the name is linked
straight to the primary reference — so you can always check the original.
